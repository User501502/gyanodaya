Infrastructure & Facilities

Smart Classrooms

All our classrooms are equipped with modern interactive smart boards and high-speed internet to facilitate digital learning.

Computer Lab

State-of-the-art computer labs with the latest hardware and software to ensure students are tech-savvy from an early age.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs that provide hands-on experience and foster scientific inquiry.

Library

A vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources in a quiet, spacious environment conducive to reading.

Sports Ground

Large playground with facilities for cricket, football, basketball, and athletics to ensure physical fitness.

Transport

Safe and reliable bus service covering all major parts of the city with GPS tracking and trained staff.
