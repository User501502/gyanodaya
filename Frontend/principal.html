Principal's Message

Mrs. Asha Sharma

Principal, Gyanodaya

"Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think."

Welcome to Gyanodaya Public School. It is my privilege to lead this institution where we strive to provide a holistic education that balances academic rigor with creative exploration and character building. Our goal is to create environment where every student feels valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

We believe in fostering a partnership between parents, teachers, and students to ensure a supportive and enriching educational journey. I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the wonderful opportunities that await your child at Gyanodaya.

Warm Regards,
Mrs. Asha Sharma
