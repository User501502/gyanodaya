Our Curriculum

Primary & Middle School

Our primary curriculum is designed to be engaging and exploratory, focusing on foundational literacy and numeracy. As students move to middle school, we introduce more specialized subjects to broaden their horizons.

Senior Secondary (Class XI & XII)

We offer major streams in specialized subjects:

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Computer Science, PE.

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics, PE.

Co-Curricular Activities

We believe in holistic development. Our students participate in music, dance, sports, debating, and art projects regularly to develop their talents beyond academics.
