Admission Procedure

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Registration

Fill out the online application form or visit the school office for a registration form.

2

Interaction / Assessment

For primary classes, an informal interaction. For middle/senior school, a basic aptitude assessment.

3

Documentation

Submission of Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate (original), Previous Marksheets, and Aadhar copy.

4

Admission Confirmation

Payment of admission fees to secure the seat.
